About Me

Engineering leader with a builder's mindset

I'm a Software Development Manager at Amazon with over 15 years of experience building high-scale systems and high-performing teams across healthcare, scientific computing, and e-commerce.

My journey — from GE Healthcare to MathWorks to Amazon — has given me a rare breadth of perspective. I've led teams working on mission-critical medical software, developer tools used by millions of scientists, and some of the most complex distributed infrastructure on the planet.

Through this site, I share what I've learned about engineering leadership, distributed systems, organizational design, and the craft of building great software and great teams.
